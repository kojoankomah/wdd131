Kojo's Dev Portfolio

Site Purpose:

The site serves as a professional portfolio for Kojo, providing an overview of his skills, projects, and experiences in software development and information technology. Visitors will be able to explore completed projects, review Kojo’s technical expertise, and find contact details for potential collaboration or hiring opportunities.

Scenarios:

  • How can I view Kojo’s past web development projects?
  • What technologies and programming languages does Kojo specialize in?
  • How can I contact Kojo for freelance or job opportunities?
  • Can I download Kojo’s resume from the site?
