Cape Coast

Beach of Cape Coast

Cape Coast, located in Ghana's Central Region, is a city rich in history and culture. The city is known for its vibrant fishing community, beautiful beaches, and its role as a center for education in Ghana, housing prestigious schools like Wesley Girls' High School. Cape Coast offers a blend of historical significance, natural beauty, and a welcoming atmosphere that reflects Ghana’s rich cultural heritage.
